# Areas for Improvement / Future Work

* **TUI Implementation**: The main feature of the project, the interactive TUI, is not yet implemented. This will be the next major step to realize the project's full vision.
* **Expanded Mermaid Support**: Remaining high-value features include Mermaid styling/classes (`style`, `classDef`, `:::`), additional diagram types, and nested subgraphs.
* **Rendering Polish**: Continue tightening junction/overlap edge-cases (especially around subgraph titles/borders and dense LR/RL graphs).
* **Debugging Tools**: Expand debug surfaces beyond `--debug-layout` (e.g., portal slots, subgraph envelopes, and routed segment dumps).
