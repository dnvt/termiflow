# decisions/

Durable decision records. Each file captures a single architectural or design decision.

Naming: `DEC-NNN-title.md`

## Format

```markdown
# DEC-NNN: Title

**Status:** Proposed | Decided | Deferred | Superseded
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Decider:** who
**Context:** why this decision is needed
**Decision:** what was chosen and why
**Consequences:** what changes, deferred, or becomes riskier
```
