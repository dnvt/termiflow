# Maestro Ingest Guide

`/maestro:ingest` turns external findings into structured internal decisions.

## Intake Standard

Every ingest should answer:

- what the source says
- how strong the evidence is
- why it matters or does not matter here
- what decision follows

## Decision Outcomes

Use one of these outcomes:

- `INTEGRATE`
- `MONITOR`
- `REDUNDANT`
- `OFF-SCOPE`

The value of ingest is not the summary. The value is the decision and the
reason it was made.
