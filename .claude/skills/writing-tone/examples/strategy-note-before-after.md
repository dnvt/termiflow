# Writing Tone Example — Strategy Note Before / After

## Before

We should potentially consider leaning further into the workflow
layer because there may be some meaningful opportunity there for
helping teams adopt AI in a more effective way over time.

## After

We should lean into the workflow layer. That is where teams
actually fail or win with AI adoption. Faster tickets are not the
point. Better ways of working are.

## What Changed

- hedging removed
- subject made explicit
- claim sharpened
- rhythm tightened
- abstract phrasing replaced with a concrete contrast
