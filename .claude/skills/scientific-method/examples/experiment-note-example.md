# Experiment Note — Weekly Research Pulse For Partner Discovery

**Hypothesis:** A lightweight weekly research pulse will surface
better partnership leads than ad hoc searching.

**Intervention:** Run one focused weekly research scan for
adjacent partners, then ingest and classify every finding.

**Expected Observation:** Within four weeks, the team will have a
shortlist of leads that map more directly to active priorities
than the current ad hoc list.

**Actual Observation:** Three of the first six surfaced leads
connected directly to active plans, and two replaced lower-signal
backlog targets.

**Conclusion:** Continue the pulse. It is producing stronger
inputs than unstructured searching, but the taxonomy should be
tightened to avoid generic market noise.

## Evidence Notes

- **Strongest evidence:** real leads mapped to active plans
- **Weakest assumption:** whether this quality advantage holds for
  another month
- **Decision trigger:** if two consecutive pulses fail to produce
  actionable inputs, redesign or stop
